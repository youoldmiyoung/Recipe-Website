Onion Tart

Home

This is a recipe for onion tart..

Ingredients

  • 500 g shortcrust pastry
  • 500 g onions, thinly sliced
  • 50 ml oil
  • 30 g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 ml milk
  • 200 ml cream
  • 5-10 g cumin, according to taste
  • salt, pepper, nutmeg
  • 100g "lardons", blanched

Instructions

  1. In the oil, sweat the onions to a very light golden brown, leave to cool.
  2. Thoroughly mix the milk, cream, eggs, cumin, flour, and seasoning.
  3. Line a pie tin or flan ring with the shortcrust pastry.
  4. Sprinkle the "lardons" and the onions over the bottom of the flan.
  5. Pour over the egg custart and cook for 30 minutes at 190 C (375 F)
  6. Serve very hot.
