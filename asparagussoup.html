Asparagus Soup Recipe

Asparagus Soup

Home

This is a recipe for a simple asparagus soup.

Ingredients

  • 1lb asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 small shallot, finely diced
  • 2 tsp powdered chicken bouillon
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tbsp sweet Marsala port
  • 1/4 c heavy cream
  • salt, to taste
  • white or black pepper, to taste
  1. Cut asparagus into bite sized pieces (1 inch)
  2. In a saucepan over medium-low heat, melt butter. Add shallot and cook gently until soft and translucent, no browning.
  3. Add asparagus, bouillon, and water. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook 8-12 minutes until asparagus is tender but still green.
  4. Turn off heat. Stir in Marsala and warm for 1 minute.
  5. Add salt to taste.
  6. Garnish individual bowls with splash of cream and fresh peppper.
  7. Optional: thin shavings of Parmigiano cheese.
