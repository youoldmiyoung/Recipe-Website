Sourdough English Muffins

Home

This is a recipe for sourdough english muffins.

Ingredients

  • 120 g active sourdough starter
  • 240 g milk (room temp or slightly warm)
  • 30 g honey
  • 28 g butter (melted and cooled)
  • 360 g all-purpose flour
  • 6 g salt
  • cornmeal or semolina for dusting

Instructions

  1. In a bowl mix starter, mil, honey, butter. Add flour and salt, stir until shaggy.
  2. Knead gently, 3-5 minutes. Dough should be soft, slightly tacky, not sticky.
  3. Cover and rest 6-10 hours at room temp or overnight in fridge (8-12 hours). Dough should be puffy, 50-75% risen, with bubbles visible under the surface.
  4. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface and roll until 3/4 inch thick.
  5. Cut with biscuit cutter or glass.
  6. Dust tops and bottoms with cornmeal and place on parchment or tray.
  7. Rest 45-75 minutes, covered (they should look slightly puffy, not doubled).
  8. Preheat cast-iron skillet or griddle on low. Lightly grease pan.
  9. Cook muffins 5-7 mins per side.
  10. Cool at least 20 minutes.
  11. Fork split, not knife cut!
